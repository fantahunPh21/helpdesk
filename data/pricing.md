# Pricing FAQ

## What are the pricing tiers?

We offer three pricing tiers to suit different needs:

**Starter** - $29/month
- Up to 100 support tickets per month
- Email support
- Basic knowledge base access
- Community forum access

**Professional** - $99/month
- Up to 1,000 support tickets per month
- Priority email and chat support
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- Custom branding options
- API access

**Enterprise** - Custom pricing
- Unlimited support tickets
- 24/7 phone and dedicated support
- Custom integrations
- SLA guarantees
- On-premise deployment options

## What's included in each plan?

All plans include access to our core helpdesk features: ticket management, knowledge base, and basic reporting. Professional and Enterprise plans unlock advanced features like custom workflows, integrations, and priority support.

## Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate the difference.

## Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes, we offer 20% off when you commit to an annual plan. Contact our sales team for details.
