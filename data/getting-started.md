# Getting Started Guide

## Welcome to HelpDesk AI!

This guide will help you set up your account and start using our platform in minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit our website and click "Sign Up". Enter your email address and create a secure password. You'll receive a confirmation email—click the link to verify your account.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Workspace

After logging in, you'll be prompted to create your first workspace. Give it a name that represents your team or organization. You can create multiple workspaces if needed.

## Step 3: Get Your API Key

To integrate HelpDesk AI with your applications, you'll need an API key. Go to Settings > API Keys and click "Generate New Key". Copy this key and store it securely—you'll need it for integrations.

## Step 4: Configure Your Knowledge Base

Upload your documentation and FAQs to build your knowledge base. Go to Knowledge Base > Add Articles and start uploading markdown or text files. Our AI will automatically index and make them searchable.

## Step 5: Create Your First Ticket Category

Set up ticket categories that match your support workflow. Go to Settings > Ticket Categories and create categories like "Billing", "Technical Support", "Feature Requests", etc.

## Step 6: Invite Your Team

Go to Team > Members and invite your team members by email. Assign roles (Admin, Agent, Viewer) based on their responsibilities.
